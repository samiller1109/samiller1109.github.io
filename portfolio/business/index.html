The World’s Oldest Businesses

A SQL and Python Data Storytelling Project

Project Summary

This project explores the longevity of businesses using data from BusinessFinancing.co.uk. It analyzes the industries, continents, and trends of the oldest still-operating companies, some founded before 1000 AD.

Interactive Timeline

This timeline visualizes companies founded before 1000 AD, color-coded by name. Each dot represents one business, and the key (legend) helps you identify them.

Exploratory Insights

Founding Year Range

The dataset spans from 578 AD to the 20th century.

Oldest Company and Industry

The oldest company is Kongō Gumi, a construction firm founded in Japan in 578 AD.

Most Common Industries

Hospitality and manufacturing are the dominant industries among the world’s oldest companies.

Oldest by Continent

Key Takeaways

  • Many of the oldest companies operate in stable, essential industries.
  • Geography plays a role: Europe and Asia are home to most of these businesses.
  • Long-standing tradition and family ownership are common themes.

Technology Used

SQL: JOINs, aggregations, filtering
Python: Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
Platform: Google Colab, SQLite

SELECT b.business, b.year_founded, c.category
FROM businesses b
JOIN categories c ON b.category_code = c.category_code
ORDER BY b.year_founded ASC
LIMIT 1;
